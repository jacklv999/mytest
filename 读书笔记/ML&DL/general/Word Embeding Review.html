  • LDA

    In LDA, every document will be considered to be related with some alternative topic, and those topic were known by us in prior.

    the prior is Dirichlet distribution, simply speak,

    • In document level, there are only several important topic, and most other topic are unimportant.
    • In word level, only some of highly related words appear in high frequency, and the other word are in low frequency.

    Then we have LDA model.

3. Fixed Word Embedding

  • Word2vec

    Skip-gram with negative sample has been equal to a global sparse pointwise mutal information matrix factorization. But there are some different between MF and word2vec:

    • in word similar, SVD and Word2vec are similar;
    • word analogies, word2vec is much better than SVD.

    There are two method to compute word embedding:

    • Skip-gram: Using the context to predict word
    • COBW: Using the word to predict the context;

    this two method were proof to be same in NN situation.

    Note: In the train process, negative sampling and hierarchical softmax were used for faster trainning.

Tips for Word Embedding Training

  • More dimension will have better result;
  • Unrelated corpus will reduce its performance, try to use the corpus only come from the specific domain;
  • Large word window learns more topic info, and small window learns more about word function and context;
