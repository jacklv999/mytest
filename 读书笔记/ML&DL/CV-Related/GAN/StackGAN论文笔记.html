2. Introduction

Background

Difficult to gen. high-res img from text

  • adding more upsampling layer only results in training instability
  • Img distrib and GANs distrib may not overlap (worsen as img res goes up)
  • Unable to get high-res img without additional annotation(why stage II)
Our StackGAN

Decomposing text to img synthesis into two tractable sub-problems

  • Stage- I GAN: gen. low-res img at first
  • Stage-II GAN: gen. high-res img on the Stage-I result and the text again
Conditioning Augmentation

Limit training text-img pairs result in sparsity in text conditioning manifold
