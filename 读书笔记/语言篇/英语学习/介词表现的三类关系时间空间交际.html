介词By补充

一、POSITION OR PLACE

1. PREP Someone or something that is by something else is beside it and close to it. 在…旁边

2. ADV By is also an adverb. 在旁边

3. PREP If a person or vehicle goes by you, they move past you without stopping. 经过

4. ADV By is also an adverb. 经过 [ADV after v]

例：The bomb went off as a police patrol went by.

炸弹在巡警队经过时爆炸了。

5. PREP If you stop by a place, you visit it for a short time. 暂时 (拜访)

6. ADV By is also an adverb. 暂时地

例：I'll stop by after dinner and we'll have that talk.

晚餐后我会过来待会儿，我们可以谈谈那件事。

二、TIMES AND AMOUNTS

1. PREP If something happens by a particular time, it happens at or before that time. 到…时; 在…之前

例：By eight o'clock he had arrived at my hotel.

他八点前已到了我住的旅馆。

2. PREP You use by to talk about measurements of area. For example, if a room is twenty feet by fourteen feet, it measures twenty feet in one direction and fourteen feet in the other direction. 表示面积尺寸 [PREP num]

例：Three prisoners were sharing one small cell 3 metres by 2½ metres.

3名囚犯共住一间3米长2.5米宽的小牢房。




一、用于人名或人称代词，表示主客体之间的某种关系。

1.Dinner is on me. 这顿饭我请。

2.You're still into Monica. 你还是对Monica念念不忘。

3.It’s a secret between you and me. 这是你我之间的秘密。

4.I’m behind you. 我支持你。

5.It is beyond me why they ever got married at all. 我不明白他们当初为什么要结婚。

6.Calculus was always beyond me. 微积分对我来说太难了。

7.He’s under Jerry. 他是Jerry手下的人。

8.He is above me. 他是我的顶头上司。

9.The FBI is after me for fraud. 因为诈骗，联邦调查局正在追捕我。

10.It is beneath him to tell a lie. 说谎有失他的身份。

二、方位介词加抽象名词，表示处于某种状态或某种关系。

11.I’m in a hurry. 我很急。

12.I’m on a diet. 我在节食。

13.Who is the lady in white? 穿白衣服的那位小姐是谁？

14.What do you do at work? 你上班干些什么？

15.I read the newspaper over breakfast. 我边吃早饭边看报纸。

16.The whole class is in a heated discussion. 全班同学正在热烈讨论。

17.Listen,since we're on the subject... 既然我们谈到了这个话题…

18.Just write a letter how great I am, try and keep it under a page, if you can.就是写写我有多好之类的，尽你所能，不要超过一页纸。

19.You just can’t walk out on the bill. That’s illegal. 你不能不付钱就走，这是违法的。

20.I know the idea of choosing friendship over sex seems like the last thing any guywants to hear. 男人都不愿意听什么要重友轻色之类的话。

三、on, off, in, out, up，down，through等空间介词转化为形容词或副词。

21.Do you know what’s on tonight? 你知道今晚有什么节目吗？

22.Carol is off for the whole week. Carol这周全休。

23.I’m afraid the wedding is off. 恐怕婚礼取消了。

24.You’re never in when I call. 我每次打电话你都不在。

25.Longhair is in again. 长头发又开始流行了。

26.If the ball hits you, you’re out. 要是球打到你，你就出局了。

27.Our computers are down right now so I can’t give you an account balance. 我们的电脑出故障了，所我们不能为你提供账户余额信息。

28.We were down by 17 points at half-time. 半场结束，我们落后17分。

29.I need to use the computer when you’re through. 等你用完电脑我想用一下。

30.As much as I hate to admit it, I think we’re through. 尽管一百个不愿意，但我还得承认我们的关系完结了。

四、用介词表达动作

31.Are the kids still up? 孩子们还没睡吗？

32.Her flight is not in yet. 她的航班还没有到。

33.You’re on in two minutes. 你两分钟后上场。

34.We’re off. 我们要走了。

35.We need to make plans for next week, so are you in or out? 我们要计划下一周的活动了，你参加吗？

36.She fell in love with him. 她爱上了他。

37.He was out of humor. 他丧失了幽默感。

38.Listen,since we're on the subject... =Since we’re talking about this subject.

39.He’son the phone.= He’s calling someone.

40.I pick up my sister from her school.=I go to my sister’s school and pick her up.
